In Popular Culture

Penguins are widely considered to be fairly endearing for their upright, waddling gait, swimmiing ability and lack of fear of humans with their blank-and-white plumage to be likened to a suit and tie.

Penguins have been the subject of many books, films and TV shows over the years such as Happy Feet, Surf's Up and Madagascar. There has also been a feature length documentary called March of the Penguins and several BBC documentaries including BBC Earth.


As well as books and films, penguins have been featured on on TV shows like Penguin Town, Penguins of Madagascar, Octonauts and Pingu to name a few


As well as being popular in tv and film, penguins can also offer comedic releif in the form of funny videos.
